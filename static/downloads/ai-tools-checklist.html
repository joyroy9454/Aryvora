📝 AI Writing & Essay Tools

Tools that help you write better essays, emails, and assignments.

ChatGPT Freemium
Best all-around AI assistant. Great for brainstorming, outlining, and drafting. Free tier uses GPT-4o mini.
Free / $20mo
Claude Freemium
Best for nuanced writing and analysis. Produces more thoughtful, well-structured content than ChatGPT.
Free / $20mo
Grammarly Freemium
Grammar, spelling, and tone checker. The mobile keyboard works in any app. Essential for every student.
Free / $12mo
Notion AI Freemium
AI-powered note-taking and organization. Summarize notes, generate study guides, and draft essays.
Free / $10mo
Copy.ai Freemium
Quick marketing copy, social media captions, and email templates. Great for student organizations.
Free / $39mo

Tools that help you memorize information and study more effectively.

Anki Free (Android)
The gold standard for spaced repetition. Scientifically proven to improve long-term retention.

Quizlet AI Freemium

Free alternative to Quizlet with AI features. Import Quizlet decks and generate flashcards from notes.
Free
Otter.ai Freemium

Free / $17mo
