### Chapter 1: The Research Problem AI Solves
Finding sources, organizing notes, structuring arguments — these take hours. AI tools can compress them into minutes. This chapter maps the full research workflow.

Perplexity AI for source finding. Elicit for academic papers. Scholarcy for summarization. Notion AI for organization. All free or cheap for students.
### Chapter 3: Finding and Organizing Sources
Go from a blank page to ten relevant sources in fifteen minutes. The systematic approach to AI-powered literature review.

What professors allow, what they do not, and how to use AI without crossing the line. Includes a decision flowchart.

AI as your outline partner. From thesis statement to section headers to argument flow — let AI structure, you write.

How to cite AI assistance. Tools that check for flags. The difference between AI-assisted and AI-generated.
