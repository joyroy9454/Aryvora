Reading is not studying. Highlighting is not learning. This chapter maps the complete pipeline from lecture notes to exam-ready knowledge.

Feed AI your notes and get summaries, flashcards, quizzes, and concept maps. Every format, one source.

AI generates the flashcards. Anki handles the schedule. This combination is mathematically optimal for memory.

Create custom practice exams for every subject. Time yourself. Grade yourself. Target weak spots before the real exam.
