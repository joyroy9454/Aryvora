## The AI Study System — Complete Framework {#ai-study-system}

This is the system top students use. It combines AI tools with proven learning techniques.

### Before Class
1. **Preview the material** — Ask AI to summarize upcoming topics
2. **Generate questions** — Create questions to focus on during the lecture
3. **Review prerequisites** — Use AI to explain any background concepts you missed

### During Class
1. **Record and transcribe** — Use Otter.ai or similar for searchable notes
2. **Note key concepts** — Focus on understanding, not transcription
3. **Mark unclear points** — Flag topics to review with AI later

### After Class (within 24 hours)
1. **Summarize notes** — Paste notes into AI and ask for a structured summary
2. **Generate flashcards** — Convert notes into Q&A format (15-25 cards per session)
3. **Test yourself** — Ask AI to quiz you on the material
4. **Fill gaps** — Ask AI to explain anything you did not understand

### Before Exams (2-4 weeks out)
1. **Create a study plan** — Give AI your exam date and topics, get a schedule
2. **Practice problems** — Generate and solve practice questions daily
3. **Review weak areas** — Focus on topics you consistently get wrong
4. **Final summary** — Generate a one-page review sheet for the last day

## AI Flashcards & Spaced Repetition {#flashcards}

Spaced repetition is the single most effective study technique. AI makes creating flashcards 10x faster.

### How it works
1. Paste your notes into ChatGPT or Claude
2. Ask it to generate 20-30 flashcards in Q&A format
3. Export to Anki, Knowt, or Quizlet
4. Review daily using spaced repetition

### Best Flashcard Apps

| Tool | Best For | Price | AI Features |
|------|----------|-------|-------------|
| **Anki** | Power users, med students | Free (paid iOS) | Best spaced repetition |
| **Knowt** | Students who want free AI | Free | AI generates from notes |
| **Quizlet** | Easy to use, collaboration | Free tier | AI study modes |
| **Brainscape** | Confidence-based learning | Free tier | Good for languages |

## Exam Preparation with AI {#exam-prep}

### Creating a Study Plan
Give AI: your exam date, list of topics, and how comfortable you are with each. It will create a personalized day-by-day study schedule.

### Generating Practice Questions
Upload your notes and ask AI to generate 20-30 practice questions in the style of your exam (multiple choice, short answer, essay).

### Identifying Weak Areas
After practice questions, ask AI to analyze your wrong answers and suggest which topics to focus on.

## AI Safety & Academic Ethics {#academic-integrity}

Using AI responsibly in academics is essential. Here is what you need to know:

### What is acceptable
- Using AI to understand concepts and explanations
- Asking AI to review your writing for clarity
- Using AI to generate practice questions
- Brainstorming with AI

### What is NOT acceptable
- Submitting AI-generated essays as your own work
- Using AI on closed-book exams or assignments where AI is prohibited
- Having AI write your code without understanding it
- Using AI to bypass learning objectives

### AI Detection
Tools like Turnitin and GPTZero can detect AI-generated content. Learn how they work so you understand the risks.
