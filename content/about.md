# About AI Blog Factory

**AI Blog Factory** is a digital publication creating premium guides, eBooks, and practical resources for students, creators, and tech learners.

Founded and run by **Joy Roy** — a BSc Data Science student who builds things with AI while studying, not after graduating.

Hey, I'm **Joy**. I'm a 2nd-semester BSc Data Science student who calls himself a "vibe coder" — someone who uses AI tools to build real things fast.

## My Philosophy

1. **Practical over theoretical.** Every product includes steps you can use today.
2. **Beginner-friendly.** No jargon without explanation.
3. **Honest.** I only recommend tools and methods I've actually tested.
4. **AI-assisted, not AI-dependent.** I use AI to research, draft, and refine — but every piece is reviewed and improved by a human (me).
