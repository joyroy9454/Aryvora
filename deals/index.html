How to Get More Deals#

Pro Tips for Students#

  1. Always check the education store. Every major brand has one. Search “[brand] student discount” before buying anything.

  2. Use your .edu email. Many deals just require a .edu email address. No verification needed.

  3. Claim GitHub Education first. It unlocks dozens of other deals.

  4. Check for bundle deals. Spotify + Hulu, Microsoft 365, Amazon Prime — bundles save more than individual subscriptions.

  5. Use browser extensions for automatic coupons. Honey, Capital One Shopping, and RetailMeNot check for student discounts automatically.

  6. Ask at the campus bookstore. Many stores offer discounts on software, hardware, and accessories that are not advertised online.

  7. Check IsThereAnyDeal.com for software and game deals.

  8. Follow r/StudentDeals on Reddit for community-shared offers.
